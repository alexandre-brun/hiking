Hiking in France

Hiking in France offers breathtaking landscapes, from the towering peaks of the French Alps to the rugged coastlines of Brittany. Explore the iconic Mont Blanc Massif, wander the enchanting vineyards of Alsace, or trek through the majestic Verdon Gorge. France's diverse trails provide unforgettable adventures for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Discover Hiking

Where to hike in France ?

Mont Blanc Massif

Offers a range of trails, including the famous Tour du Mont Blanc.

GR34 in Brittany

Scenic coastal path with stunning cliffs and charming villages.

Verdon Gorge

Known as the "Grand Canyon of Europe," it offers breathtaking views and cave exploration.

Pyrenees National Park

A vast mountain range with diverse trails and picturesque landscapes.

Vanoise National Park

France's first national park, known for its alpine meadows, glaciers, and wildlife.

Corsica GR20

Challenging long-distance trail that crosses Corsica's rugged mountains.

Alsace Wine Route

Beautiful Mediterranean coastal hikes near Marseille.
